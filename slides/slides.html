Why Build This?

Human Dignity

Shopping should feel normal. Subsidies are applied discreetly in the app, not at a stigmatizing "aid counter".

Security

Refugees are vulnerable targets. By using ZK proofs, we ensure their location and identity data is never stored on our servers.

Efficiency

Programmable money (Crypto) ensures funds are spent on essentials, reducing fraud and administrative overhead.
